A PenguinMod extension that lets you take full control of the sequencer so you can fix all of those pesky one-frame-off bugs.

URL: [`https://raw.githubusercontent.com/the-can-of-soup/pm_threads/refs/heads/main/soup_threads.js`](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/the-can-of-soup/pm_threads/refs/heads/main/soup_threads.js)<br>

**_This extension must be loaded unsandboxed, and does not support TurboWarp!_**
